    How to Play

    To play this game, begin by downloading the zip file. For Android, download APK software. On PC, run the executable. Follow the READMDE for more instructions.

    Objectives

    The objective of this game is to eliminate all your opponents before they eliminate you. Health decreases every time a player is hit. Upon reaching zero health, the player is removed from the map. Health can be regained by driving through power-ups.

    Mobile (Android) Instructions

    Move: Left Analog Stick
    Shoot: Shoot Button
    Camera Switch: Swipe Left (Top Right Corner)

    PC Instructions

    Move: Arrow Keys
    Shoot: 'P'
    Camera Switch: 'v'

    Technical Details & Credits

    The game was created in Unity. MadDash was started while in professor Ming Chow's 0023 Game Development class at Tufts Univerisity.
