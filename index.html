How to Play

To play this game, begin by downloading the zip file which contains the executables for PC and Android along with the source code. For Android, transfer the .apk file to your Android device and install. For PC, run the .exe file. See the README file for more information.

Objectives

The objective of this game is to eliminate all your opponents before they eliminate you. Health decreases every time a player is hit by a bullet. Upon reaching zero health, the player is removed from the map. Health can be regained by driving through the wrench power up. The other power up available is the fire ball. Once three has been collected, the player can call in an asteroid strike.

Mobile (Android) Instructions

Move: Left Analog Stick
Shoot: Shoot Button
Camera Switch: Swipe left from the right upper quarter of the screen Call for Asteroid strike: Swipe down from the right upper quarter of the screen

PC Instructions

Move: WASD keys Shoot: 'P'
Camera Switch: 'v' Call for Asteroid strike: 'o'
